GAME RULES

  • 1. The Game is Degined in such a way that you will earn a point on eating the stuff and the size of the snake increase by one point.
  • 2. Use Arrow Keys to move the Snake.
  • 3. In Case,if the head of the snake touches the body of the snake it will be result in gameover.
  • 4. If the head of snake bump into the wall it will be gameover.
  • 5. The Difficulty seletion toggle is to select the speed of the snake. Higher the difficulty greater the speed.
